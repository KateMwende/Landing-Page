About

My sister had/has literally the most beautiful nails. I remember as a kid I always wanted to grow up fast so that I could eventually see how my nails would look like, when they'd taken their true form and I'm actually allowed to grow them long. To say the least i was obsessed! And from then on that's when I knew I had a passion for making nails look beautiful. And with that I started my small business in 2019, Color Couture. My project is inspired by my small business. Wanting it to have something to show for itself in the near future and it having a seamless appointment booking method is what really pushed me to pick this as my project. We had a timeline of three weeks to complete everything.
